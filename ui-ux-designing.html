information Architecture

Information Architecture in UI/UX Designing refers to the structural design of your website. It is the foundation on which your site or app is built and includes elements such as navigation, organization, labelling, and searchability. IA is a crucial part of the design process and can make or break the user experience. A well-designed help users find the information they need quickly and easily, while a poorly designed IA can leave them feeling frustrated and lost.
